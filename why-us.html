Our Story

42 West Design was born in Miami in 2010, founded by creative director Alexandro West with a single conviction: that world-class design shouldn't be reserved for Fortune 500 companies. Starting from a studio apartment in Wynwood, Alex and a team of three began crafting brand identities and websites for ambitious local businesses who wanted to punch above their weight class.

The results spoke for themselves. Within two years, 42 West had become the go-to design partner for Miami's most exciting startups and established brands alike. As our reputation grew, so did our reach — in 2014, we opened our New York studio in the Flatiron District, and in 2018, we expanded to San Diego's vibrant East Village. Today, our 35-person team spans three studios and two time zones, united by one mission.

That mission is simple: to be the most trusted creative partner for brands that want to grow. We've completed over 500 projects, won 23 industry awards, and most importantly, helped our clients generate millions in incremental revenue through the power of strategic design. We measure our success by yours — and we're just getting started.
